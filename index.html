dai Mould

Welcome to Remora Dai Mould, a leading engineering company based in Morbi, India. With over 19+ of experience in the industry, we have established ourselves as a trusted and reliable source of high-quality ceramic products.

At Remora Dai Mould, we are dedicated to producing premium ceramics that are both functional and aesthetically pleasing. Our state-of-the-art manufacturing facility in Morbi is equipped with the latest technology and machinery, ensuring that every product that leaves our facility meets the highest standards of quality and craftsmanship.

We believe in providing exceptional customer service and building long-lasting relationships with our clients. Our commitment to quality, affordability, and timely delivery sets us apart from our competitors and has made us a trusted and reliable partner for businesses and individuals alike.
